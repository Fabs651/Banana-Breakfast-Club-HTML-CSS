The Banana & Cinnamon Musli

Start the day with a nutritious musli for powerful days:

  1. Pour 100 grams of oats into a bowl
  2. Add 50 grams of chia seeds, flaxseeds, cacao nibs and some cinnamon
  3. Add water or milk to cover the surface and let soak for 10 minutes
  4. Cut 2 bananas in pieces and add it to the bowl
